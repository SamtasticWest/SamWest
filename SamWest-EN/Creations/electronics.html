Electronic Creations

With over ten years of experience in electronics, both personally and professionally, I have ventured into creating and manufacturing various electronic solutions. My expertise includes assembling complex systems, programming circuits, and designing innovative devices tailored to different needs.

Computers and Systems

My interest in computers began when I was under 10 years old, and since then, I have built computers for myself, businesses, and others. Building computers has always been a passion of mine, and it has allowed me to gain extensive experience in assembling, customizing, and optimizing computer systems. I have worked on various projects, from workstations for professionals to setups for home users.

School Projects

Here are two projects completed during my university studies that demonstrate my expertise in electronics and manufacturing.

GRBL-based CNC Machine

This project was my final project in college, where I built a CNC machine using the GRBL controller. This project allowed me to develop in-depth skills in programming, mechatronics, and automated systems fabrication.

Illuminated Medicinal Wheel

During my university studies, I created an illuminated medicinal wheel for one of my courses. This project combined my interest in electronics and cultural traditions. I designed and built this wheel using LEDs and a custom circuit to provide dynamic and meaningful lighting.

Illuminated Comic Covers

One of my recent creations includes illuminated comic covers. I made these covers using both transparent and opaque plastics to create captivating light effects. These covers add a modern touch to classic pieces, integrating electronic elements for an interactive experience.
