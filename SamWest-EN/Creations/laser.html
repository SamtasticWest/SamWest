Laser Engraving Creations

A few years after university, I became interested in laser engraving and cutting, which turned into both a passion and a business activity. I use my laser cutting machine to engrave designs on various materials, including wood and acrylic. These creations vary, ranging from video game and comic book covers to custom designs. I keep some works for my personal collection, and others are for sale.
